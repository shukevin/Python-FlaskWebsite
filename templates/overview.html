Steam Makers

Site Overview

In this website you'll find our curated searches for all your Steam gaming needs. We hope to provide you the ability to make informed decisions on your purchases and remove any feelings of buyer's remorse.

Above, you will find a navigation bar to the current searches available with our database. We thank you for your support by visiting our website.

What does each search do?

Popularity

Popularity finds the highest net rating(positive ratings-negative ratings) game developer on steam. You are able to limit the search by number of games developed, and results shown.

Median Price

Median Price finds the game developers with the highest/lowest game pricings. You are able to limit the search by price ranges, and order the prices by highest/lowest.

Best Value

Best value finds game titles with the highest/lowest playtime to net ratings(positive ratings-negative ratings). You are able to limit the search by average playtime and net rating count.

Rated R

Rated R finds the ratings and price of ESBR 'R' rated titles. You are able to limit the search by inputting a minimum rating count.

Requirements

Requirements finds the game developer OS requirements for game titles. You are able to limit the search by language, genre, and platform(Windows, Mac, Linux).
